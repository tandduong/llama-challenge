The Era since 40 million years...

From the rolling hills of the Andes to the wilderness of northern America, originated as a friend of the mountains until appearing in human stories, contributing to the formation of human civilization for millions of years and become the symbol of a country, that is the amazing adventure of our family - Llamas...

Scroll Down

Before entering the myth of the beautiful country of Peru, our family was one of the indispensable factors in the life of the mountainous people in South America. We accompany the farmers on challenging journeys, bringing children warmth in the winter, and an endless source of inspiration for artists and strangers from remote lands. We teach children about friendliness, perseverance and use their strength in the things that bring happiness to life.

Our ancestors made their mark on this planet tens of millions of years ago, and like other animals, our descendants made migrations to new lands, from the far north to cold Europe. But wherever we are, we still keep the traditional traits in quality: hard work, perseverance, and friendliness. Some people who live with us realize these values ​​and they have decided to apply them to their work environment and lifestyle, for example, the shoe company Zappos.

Members of our great family were born in the mountains, received the sweet fruits donated by the forest god, carried on the shoulders of long trips and were close friends of humans for centuries. And if you don't believe it, come to us in Peru, and you'll get the answer ...

Here is a little about me.

I am an international student from Vietnam and have a deep interest in programming. I love building and designing websites. I see myself as an introvert and quiet person, but friendly and open-minded.

My favorite subjects when I was in high school are Math and History, and I chose Computer Science to study because of my sister, and also because of my love for interacting with software and coding. I have taught myself HTML and CSS when I was a freshman before I enrolled in the web-based class, where I learned more about JavaScript and ReactJs. In this project, I used HTML5 and CSS3 for most of the visual design, I built the single-page app because I think viewers could be easier to keeping track of the information provided there. I also grabbed a little JavaScript and Ajax for the effect of the webpage: the toggle navigation bar, the auto-filled color effect of the nav bar when scrolling down, and the smooth links.

I love sketching, making arts and coding so I decided to be the Fornt-End Developers, which I could combine all my hobbies in one page. Also, being a web developer is my dream when I started college life because I think it is fun and neccessary for people in nowaday business. And by doing that, I am able to learning and relaxing at the same time. "Choose a Job You Love, and You Will Never Have To Work a Day in Your Life"

I traveled to the US in 2017 to start my college career, and right now I am a Junior. I always tell myself to work hard and give 100% effort in all exams and projects to keep a good grade.
